Insurances for students

Insuring your home is really easy! You can choose from up to three packages to find the best fit for you.

Insurance package S fits for those who want the minimal price for their insurance. Our cheapest package includes responsibility insurance, which most land lords require from their tenants. With responsibility insurance you cover accidental breakages of either personal items or someone else's which you are legally bound to cover.

Insurance package M fits for those who want more comprehensive security for their personal property. M-package covers theft insurance, which covers the theft of personal property from inside your home. Breaking into student apartments is quite common which is why one should prepare for it by insuring.

Student's life insurance

Life insurance covers the insured persons death. Voluntary life insurances completes the legally bound social insurance. Although Finland's social insurance is quite comprehensive, it's benefits defining is schematic and is based on the average citizen's needs. People do however have varying living situations and needs for security, which are completed by private insurances.
